Our Mission

Our goal is to build a supportive community of digital nomads who are passionate about making a difference. We believe that by coming together, we can create a powerful network that not only benefits the individuals within it but also supports the broader mission of helping Ukraine flourish. Whether you are a seasoned nomad or just starting your remote work journey, we welcome you to join us.

Young professionals in Kyiv, Ukraine

We offer resources, insights, and connections to help you navigate the logistics of working from Ukraine. From finding reliable internet connections to understanding the local culture and regulations, we’ve got you covered. Our community members share their experiences, tips, and advice to ensure that your time in Ukraine is both productive and fulfilling.

How You Can Get Involved

Getting involved is easy! Here are some ways you can contribute to our mission:

  • Work Remotely from Ukraine: Enjoy vibrant cities and stunning nature
  • Partner with Local Businesses: Bring your skills and expertise to the local market
  • Hire Local Talent: Support the local workforce. Hire Ukrainian talent
  • Join Our Community: Connect with like-minded digital nomads
  • Spread the Word2s: Share our mission, encourage others to join the movement

Each of these actions contributes to the larger goal of supporting Ukraine’s economy and helping it thrive in the global digital landscape. Together, we can make a significant impact, one project at a time.

Discover Ukraine

Ukraine offers a wealth of experiences that go beyond work. Explore the country’s diverse regions, from the Carpathian Mountains to the Black Sea coast, and immerse yourself in the local culture. Whether you’re into history, nature, or modern city life, Ukraine has something to offer every digital nomad.
